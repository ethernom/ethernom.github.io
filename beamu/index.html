How BeamU U2F works?

Register your BeamU U2F Key

Go to the Security Settings of a supported service, select two-factor authentication using a security key, and follow setup instructions.

Authenticate on PC or Mac

Authenticate using USB on PC or Mac. This verifies that you are not a remote hacker.

Authenticate on iOS or Android

Authenticate using Bluetooth LE or NFC on your iOS or Android device.
